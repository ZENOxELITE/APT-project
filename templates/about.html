About MonarchTime

Crafting timeless masterpieces since 1985

Founded in 1985, MonarchTime has been at the forefront of luxury watchmaking for nearly four decades. What began as a small workshop in Geneva has grown into a globally recognized brand synonymous with precision, elegance, and timeless design.

Our master craftsmen combine traditional Swiss watchmaking techniques with cutting-edge technology to create timepieces that are not just instruments for telling time, but works of art that tell stories.

Every MonarchTime watch undergoes rigorous quality control and is backed by our commitment to excellence, ensuring that each piece meets the highest standards of craftsmanship and durability.
